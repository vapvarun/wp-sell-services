# Vendor Profile & Portfolio

Your vendor profile is your marketplace identity. A complete, professional profile attracts more buyers and builds trust.

## Why Your Profile Matters

A strong vendor profile:

- Increases visibility in search results
- Builds buyer confidence
- Showcases your expertise
- Differentiates you from competitors
- Improves conversion rates

## Accessing Your Profile Settings

1. Log in to your vendor account
2. Navigate to **My Account → Vendor Dashboard → Profile**
3. Or go to **My Account → Edit Profile**

![Vendor profile editor](../images/vendor-profile-editor.png)

## Profile Components

### Basic Information

Complete these essential fields:

| Field | Description | Tips |
|-------|-------------|------|
| **Display Name** | Your public vendor name | Use your business or professional name |
| **Tagline** | One-line description | Summarize your expertise (60 chars max) |
| **Profile Photo** | Your avatar image | Use professional headshot or logo (400x400px) |
| **Cover Image** | Banner background | Professional banner (1920x400px recommended) |

### About You Section

Write a compelling vendor biography:

- **Who You Are**: Your background and experience
- **What You Offer**: Services and expertise areas
- **Why Choose You**: Unique selling points
- **Professional Achievements**: Relevant credentials

**Character Limit**: 500-1000 words recommended

**Example**:
```
I'm a professional graphic designer with 8+ years of experience
creating logos, branding materials, and marketing graphics for
small businesses and startups. I specialize in modern, minimalist
designs that help brands stand out. My work has been featured in
Design Magazine and I hold a BFA in Graphic Design from State University.

I deliver high-quality work on time and offer unlimited revisions
until you're completely satisfied. Let's bring your vision to life!
```

![Vendor profile about section](../images/vendor-profile-about.png)

### Skills & Expertise

Add relevant skills to your profile:

1. Click **Add Skills**
2. Enter skill names (e.g., "Logo Design", "Adobe Illustrator")
3. Skills display as tags on your profile
4. Buyers can filter vendors by skills

**Recommended**: Add 5-10 skills that best represent your expertise.

### Languages

List languages you can communicate in:

- Select language from dropdown
- Choose proficiency level: Basic, Conversational, Fluent, Native
- Add multiple languages

This helps buyers find vendors who speak their language.

### Contact & Social Links

Connect your professional social profiles:

- **Website**: Your portfolio or business website
- **LinkedIn**: Professional profile link
- **Facebook**: Business page
- **Twitter**: Professional account
- **Instagram**: Portfolio account
- **GitHub**: For developers
- **Behance/Dribbble**: For designers

**Privacy Note**: Only links you add will be displayed publicly.

### Location

Add your location to help buyers find local vendors:

- **Country**: Required
- **City**: Optional
- **Time Zone**: Helps buyers understand your working hours

## Profile Completeness

The system tracks your profile completion percentage:

- **0-30%**: Incomplete - Limited visibility
- **31-60%**: Basic - Standard visibility
- **61-90%**: Good - Better search ranking
- **91-100%**: Complete - Maximum visibility

![Profile completeness indicator](../images/vendor-profile-completeness.png)

### How to Reach 100%

Complete all these elements:

- [ ] Profile photo
- [ ] Cover image
- [ ] Tagline
- [ ] About section (minimum 100 words)
- [ ] At least 5 skills
- [ ] At least 1 language
- [ ] Location (country)
- [ ] At least 1 social link
- [ ] At least 1 published service
- [ ] At least 1 portfolio item

## Portfolio Section

Showcase your best work to attract buyers.

### What is a Portfolio?

Your portfolio displays:

- Completed projects
- Sample work
- Case studies
- Visual examples of your services

### Adding Portfolio Items

1. Navigate to **Dashboard → Portfolio**
2. Click **Add Portfolio Item**
3. Fill in the portfolio form:

![Add portfolio item form](../images/vendor-add-portfolio-item.png)

#### Portfolio Item Fields

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Title** | Project name | Yes |
| **Description** | Project details, challenges, solutions | Yes |
| **Category** | Type of work | Yes |
| **Tags** | Relevant keywords | No |
| **Images** | Visual examples (1-10 images) | Yes |
| **Project URL** | Live project link | No |
| **Completion Date** | When you finished the project | No |
| **Client** | Client name (if allowed to share) | No |

### Portfolio Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or GIF
- **Size**: Maximum 5MB per image
- **Dimensions**: Minimum 800x600px
- **Quality**: High-resolution, clear images
- **Quantity**: 3-5 images per project ideal

### Portfolio Best Practices

1. **Show Your Best Work**: Quality over quantity
2. **Variety**: Display range of skills and styles
3. **Recent Projects**: Keep portfolio current
4. **Real Projects**: Use actual client work when possible
5. **Before/After**: Show transformation when relevant
6. **Context**: Explain the project goals and results
7. **Professional Photos**: Use clean, well-lit images

### Organizing Your Portfolio

- **Arrange Items**: Drag and drop to reorder
- **Feature Items**: Mark best projects as featured
- **Archive Old Work**: Hide outdated projects
- **Update Regularly**: Add new projects monthly

## How Your Profile Displays

### On Vendor Listing Pages

Your profile appears in vendor directories:

- Profile photo
- Display name
- Tagline
- Average rating
- Number of reviews
- Starting price
- Verification badge

Use shortcode `[wpss_vendors]` to display vendor listings.

### On Your Vendor Profile Page

Buyers see your complete profile:

- Cover image and profile photo
- About section
- Skills and languages
- Social links
- Statistics (orders completed, response time)
- Service listings
- Portfolio gallery
- Reviews and ratings

Use shortcode `[wpss_vendor_profile]` to display individual profiles.

### On Service Pages

Your profile summary appears on each service:

- Profile photo
- Display name
- Average rating
- Response time
- Verification level
- Link to full profile

## Profile Statistics

Your profile displays performance metrics:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Member Since** | Account creation date |
| **Orders Completed** | Successfully delivered orders |
| **Average Rating** | Overall star rating |
| **Response Time** | Average reply time to messages |
| **Delivery Rate** | On-time delivery percentage |
| **Verification Level** | Basic, Verified, or Pro |

These statistics build buyer confidence.

## Advanced Profile Features **[PRO]**

Pro marketplaces may offer:

- **Video Introduction**: Upload profile video
- **Certifications**: Display professional credentials
- **Custom Fields**: Additional profile information
- **Featured Profile**: Premium placement in listings
- **Profile Analytics**: Track profile views and conversions

## SEO Optimization

Optimize your profile for search:

- **Keywords**: Include relevant terms in your about section
- **Skills**: Add specific, searchable skills
- **Portfolio Descriptions**: Use detailed, descriptive text
- **Complete Profile**: Fully completed profiles rank higher

## Profile Visibility Settings

Control who can see your profile:

- **Public**: Anyone can view (default)
- **Buyers Only**: Only logged-in buyers
- **Hidden**: Profile not shown in listings

**Note**: Hidden profiles can still fulfill existing orders but won't receive new ones.

## Updating Your Profile

Keep your profile current:

- **Review Monthly**: Update skills and portfolio
- **Add New Work**: Showcase recent projects
- **Refresh About Section**: Keep information relevant
- **Update Photos**: Use current, professional images
- **Check Links**: Ensure social links work

## Tips for a Standout Profile

1. **Professional Photos**: Invest in quality images
2. **Compelling Story**: Write an engaging about section
3. **Proof of Expertise**: Show, don't just tell
4. **Active Portfolio**: Minimum 5-10 portfolio items
5. **Responsive**: Fast reply times improve your stats
6. **Consistent Branding**: Match your brand across all elements
7. **Social Proof**: Link active, professional social profiles

## Troubleshooting

### Profile Changes Not Saving

- Check for required field errors
- Ensure images meet size/format requirements
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

### Portfolio Images Not Uploading

- Verify image file size (max 5MB)
- Check file format (JPG, PNG, GIF only)
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Contact support if issues persist

### Profile Not Showing in Search

- Complete at least 80% of profile
- Publish at least one service
- Wait 24 hours for indexing
- Check profile visibility settings
