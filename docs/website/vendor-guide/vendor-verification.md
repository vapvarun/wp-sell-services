# Vendor Verification

Vendor verification builds buyer trust and increases your visibility on the marketplace. Higher verification levels unlock benefits and improve your reputation.

## Verification Levels

The marketplace uses three vendor verification levels:

### Basic (Default)

**Starting Level**: All new vendors begin as Basic.

**Criteria**:
- Approved vendor account
- Email verified
- At least one published service

**Benefits**:
- Can sell services
- Appear in search results
- Access to vendor dashboard

**Badge Display**: No verification badge

![Basic vendor profile](../images/vendor-level-basic.png)

### Verified

**Mid-Level**: Demonstrates reliability and quality service.

**Criteria** (typically):
- Completed at least 10 orders
- Maintained 4.5+ star average rating
- 90%+ on-time delivery rate
- Less than 5% order cancellation rate
- Active for at least 30 days
- Complete profile (80%+ completion)

**Benefits**:
- "Verified" badge on profile and services
- Higher placement in search results
- Increased buyer trust
- Priority in featured listings

**Badge Display**: ✓ Verified badge

![Verified vendor profile](../images/vendor-level-verified.png)

### Pro

**Top Level**: Elite vendors with proven track record.

**Criteria** (typically):
- Completed at least 50 orders
- Maintained 4.8+ star average rating
- 95%+ on-time delivery rate
- Less than 2% order cancellation rate
- Active for at least 90 days
- 100% profile completion
- Portfolio with at least 5 items
- Fast response time (under 2 hours average)

**Benefits**:
- "Pro" badge on profile and services
- Top placement in search results
- Featured in "Top Vendors" section
- Maximum buyer trust
- May qualify for special promotions
- **[PRO]** Custom commission rates (if enabled)
- **[PRO]** Advanced analytics access

**Badge Display**: ⭐ Pro badge

![Pro vendor profile](../images/vendor-level-pro.png)

## How Verification Works

### Automatic Verification

The system automatically evaluates your account:

1. **Daily Assessment**: Criteria checked every 24 hours
2. **Automatic Upgrade**: Level increases when criteria met
3. **Automatic Downgrade**: Level decreases if performance drops
4. **Email Notification**: You're notified of level changes

### Manual Verification

Site administrators can manually:

- Upgrade vendor level (bypassing automatic criteria)
- Downgrade vendor level (for policy violations)
- Lock verification level (prevent automatic changes)

## Maintaining Your Level

### Don't Lose Your Status

Maintain verification by:

1. **Deliver Quality Work**: Keep ratings high
2. **Meet Deadlines**: Maintain on-time delivery
3. **Communicate Quickly**: Fast response times
4. **Avoid Cancellations**: Minimize order cancellations
5. **Stay Active**: Regular activity on the platform
6. **Update Profile**: Keep information current

### What Causes Downgrades

Your level may decrease if:

- Average rating drops below threshold
- On-time delivery rate falls
- Cancellation rate increases
- Account becomes inactive (30+ days)
- Policy violations occur
- Disputes increase significantly

### Level Protection Period

After achieving a new level:

- **Grace Period**: 30 days to stabilize performance
- **Warning System**: Notification before downgrade
- **Recovery Time**: Chance to improve metrics

## Special Considerations

### New Vendor Waiting Period

- Cannot reach Verified level in first 30 days
- Cannot reach Pro level in first 90 days
- Even if all other criteria met

This ensures consistent, long-term quality.

### Seasonal Vendors

If you use [vacation mode](vacation-mode.md):

- Verification level maintained during vacation
- Activity requirements paused
- Level assessment resumes when active

### Marketplace Variations

Verification criteria may differ:

- Each marketplace sets its own requirements
- Some use different level names
- Additional levels may exist
- Criteria displayed in your dashboard

## Verification FAQs

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Automatic verification updates daily. You'll be upgraded within 24 hours of meeting criteria.

**Q: Can I request manual verification?**
A: Contact site support with your case. Manual verification is rare and at admin discretion.

**Q: Does verification expire?**
A: No expiration, but level decreases if performance drops below standards.

**Q: What if I disagree with a downgrade?**
A: Contact support to review your account. Provide context for any issues.

**Q: Do verification levels transfer to new accounts?**
A: No. Each account starts at Basic level. One account per person/business.

**Q: Can I hide my verification level?**
A: No. Verification badges are always visible to maintain marketplace transparency.
