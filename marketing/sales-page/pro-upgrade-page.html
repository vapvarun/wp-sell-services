Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free plugin required?
Yes. WP Sell Services Pro extends the free plugin. Both must be installed and active. Pro adds features via WordPress hooks — it never removes free functionality.
Do I still need WooCommerce with Pro?
No. Pro includes Standalone mode that runs without any e-commerce plugin. You can also use Easy Digital Downloads, FluentCart, or SureCart instead of WooCommerce.
How does licensing work?
After purchase, enter your license key in WP Sell Services → Settings → License to activate Pro features and automatic updates. One license per site (or multiple with agency licenses).
What happens if my license expires?
Your site continues working with all Pro features enabled. You won't receive plugin updates or support until you renew. No features are disabled.
Can I upgrade from Free to Pro later?
Yes. Install Pro at any time alongside the free version. All existing services, orders, vendors, and settings are preserved. No migration required.
Which payment gateways work best?
WooCommerce mode: Any WooCommerce gateway. Standalone mode: Direct Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay, or Offline. EDD/FluentCart/SureCart: Their native gateways work automatically.
What's your refund policy?
30-day money-back guarantee. If Pro doesn't meet your needs, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
