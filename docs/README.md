## How It Works

WP Sell Services turns your WordPress site into a marketplace where people sell their skills and others hire them - similar to Fiverr or Upwork.

**The basic flow:**

1. Someone creates a service listing ("I'll design your logo for $75")
2. A buyer finds it and places an order
3. They tell the seller what they need
4. The seller does the work and delivers
5. The buyer approves and leaves a review
6. Everyone gets paid

You earn a commission on every sale. The plugin handles orders, messaging, file delivery, reviews, and payouts.

**In the Free Version:**
- Service listings with packages and extras
- Order management and messaging
- Reviews and ratings
