# Becoming a Vendor

Learn how to register as a vendor on the marketplace and start selling your services.

## How to Register as a Vendor

You can become a vendor by registering on the marketplace. The registration process depends on how the site administrator has configured vendor registration.

### Registration Methods

The site may offer one of these registration approaches:

- **Open Registration**: Anyone can register as a vendor directly
- **Closed Registration**: Admin approval is required before you can start selling

Check the vendor registration page to see which method applies to your marketplace.

## Registration Process

### Step 1: Access the Registration Form

1. Navigate to the vendor registration page (typically at `/vendor-registration/`)
2. If you already have a buyer account, you can upgrade it to a vendor account
3. New users will create a fresh vendor account

![Vendor registration form](../images/vendor-registration-form.png)

### Step 2: Fill Out Required Information

Complete the registration form with:

- **Username**: Your unique login name
- **Email Address**: Valid email for verification and notifications
- **Password**: Secure password for your account
- **Business Name**: Your service provider or business name
- **Description**: Brief introduction about your services
- **Contact Information**: Phone number (optional)

### Step 3: Email Verification

After submitting the registration form:

1. Check your email inbox for a verification message
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. Your email address will be confirmed

**Important**: You must verify your email before you can start selling services.

### Step 4: Wait for Approval (If Required)

If the marketplace uses admin approval:

- You'll see a "Pending Approval" status
- The site administrator will review your application
- You'll receive an email notification when approved or rejected
- This process typically takes 1-3 business days

If auto-approval is enabled, you can start selling immediately after email verification.

## Switching from Buyer to Vendor

Already have a buyer account? You can upgrade to vendor status:

1. Log in to your existing account
2. Navigate to **My Account → Become a Vendor**
3. Complete the vendor registration form
4. Wait for approval if required

Your existing order history and account details remain intact.

## What You Can Do Once Approved

After your vendor account is approved, you gain access to:

- **Vendor Dashboard**: Manage all your selling activities
- **Create Services**: List services for buyers to purchase
- **Receive Orders**: Accept and fulfill buyer orders
- **Manage Portfolio**: Showcase your previous work
- **View Earnings**: Track income and request withdrawals
- **Respond to Buyer Requests**: Bid on jobs posted by buyers
- **Message Buyers**: Communicate about order requirements

## Tips for Getting Approved

If your marketplace requires admin approval:

- Provide a complete and professional profile description
- Use a real business email address
- Clearly describe the services you plan to offer
- Include relevant skills and experience
- Respond promptly to any admin questions

## Troubleshooting

### Not Receiving Verification Email

- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you entered the correct email address
- Wait 5-10 minutes for delivery
- Contact site support to resend the verification email

### Application Rejected

- Review the rejection reason provided by the admin
- Update your profile information
- Resubmit your application
- Contact site support for clarification

### Cannot Access Registration Page

- Ensure vendor registration is enabled on the site
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Contact site support for assistance
